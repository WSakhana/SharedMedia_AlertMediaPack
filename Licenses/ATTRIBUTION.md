# Media attribution

Alert Media Pack includes selected sound files from the WeakAuras 5.21.1 package, originally located in `WeakAuras/Media/Sounds`.

Only files listed as Creative Commons CC0 1.0 or Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 in WeakAuras' `Provided by.txt` are bundled here.
